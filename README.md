# IPV Attitude Predictor - linear_regression_model

**Mission:** My mission is to create equitable health for children and adolescent girls in disadvantaged communities by advocating for their rights and educating them about Sexual and Reproductive Health (SRH) and body autonomy while also equipping them with essential cybersecurity knowledge to protect their digital privacy, safety, and voice in an increasingly connected world.

**Problem:** So many factors that justify intimate partner violence (IPV) are risk factors are the reason why women and girls are not safe. This model predicts the percentage of a demographic group in a given country that agrees that IPV is justified, so that advocacy and education about SRH rights and body autonomy is carried out where violence is normalised and has a high percentage.

**Dataset:** [violence_data.csv.zip](https://github.com/user-attachments/files/30351937/violence_data.csv.zip)  DHS (Demographic and Health Survey)data on intimate partner violence, covering 70 countries (2000–2018). Source: [DHS Program survey compilation, as distributed via Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/code/gpreda/violence-against-women-and-girls).
